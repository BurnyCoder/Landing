## Definitions
- Systems science, also referred to as systems research, or, simply, [[systems]], is a [[Transdisciplinarity|transdisciplionary]] field concerned with understanding systems—from simple to complex—in nature, [[society]], [[cognition]], [[engineering]], [[technology]] and [[science]] itself. The field is diverse, spanning the [[formal science|formal]], [[Natural science|natural]], [[social science|social]], and [[Applied science|applied]] sciences.
